Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling us at +1 (234) 567-8900, using our online booking form, or visiting our clinic during office hours.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our staff will help you verify your benefits and understand your coverage.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

For dental emergencies, call our 24/7 hotline at +1 (234) 567-8911. We provide emergency dental care for issues like severe toothache, broken teeth, or injuries.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We follow strict infection control protocols including enhanced cleaning, air purification, mandatory masks, and social distancing in our waiting areas.
